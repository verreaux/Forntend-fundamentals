VANILA CAKE

This moist single-layer vanilla cake has a delicate texture and delectable crumb, but it's the old-fashioned browned-butter glaze that gives it a nutty flavor. All you need is a few ingredients and tools (mainly an 8-inch cake pan)

Vanilla Cake is one of my all-time favorite CLASSIC cake recipes, made with vanilla flavoring and topped with my go-to homemade buttercream frosting . You can also top this cake with my chocolate frosting or cream cheese frosting for more variation. We like to serve this for parties, because it’s easy to make and decorate for any occasion!

COOK TIME : 0 hours 30 mins

TOTAL TIME : 0 hours 55 mins

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 c. sifted cake flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 c. unsalted butter
  • 1 c. sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/2 c. whole milk

Driections

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees F 🥵.
  2. Lightly coat an 8-inch cake pan with butter and dust with all-purpose flour 🥘.
  3. Sift the cake flour, baking powder, and salt into a large mixing bowl 🍲.
  4. Beat in the butter one heaping 1/4 teaspoonful at a time, using an electric mixer set on low speed, until the mixture resembles coarse sand.
  5. Beat in the sugar a tablespoon at a time, until the mixture resembles fine damp sand 🍬.
  6. Beat in the eggs one at a time 🥚.
  7. Add the vanilla and milk, and beat on medium-high, just until blended 🥛.
  8. Do not overbeat.
  9. Pour into the prepared pan and bake until a wooden skewer inserted in the center comes out clean—30 to 35 minutes ⏳.
  10. Cool cake in the pan on a wire rack for 5 minutes ⌛.
  11. Un-mold and cool completely. Ice with Browned-Butter Glaze 😋.

Note : Before you begin, check baking powder expire date. If it has passed,buy a fresh canister
